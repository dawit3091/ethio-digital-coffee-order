WHO WE ARE

A place where every sip tells a story

ETHIOBUNA was born from a deep love for Ethiopian coffee culture — the birthplace of coffee itself. We opened our doors to share that culture with our community: the ritual, the aroma, the warmth of gathering around a fresh brew.

We are not just a coffee shop. We are a place to slow down, connect, and experience coffee the way it was meant to be — ceremoniously.

WHAT SETS US APART
🌍

Ethiopian Sourced

Every bean we use is sourced directly from Ethiopian highlands — Yirgacheffe, Sidama, and Harrar — where the world's finest natural-process coffees grow.

🫖

Authentic Ceremony

We serve the full three-round Ethiopian coffee ceremony — Abol, Tona, and Baraka — prepared by hand, exactly as it has been for generations.

🍞

Fresh & Local Food

Our kitchen serves freshly baked goods and Ethiopian snacks daily — the perfect companion to your coffee, made with care every morning.

🤝

Community First

ETHIOBUNA is a gathering space. Whether you are here to work, meet friends, or simply breathe — you are always welcome at our table.

OUR PROMISE

Quality you can taste, hospitality you can feel

We promise to never rush your experience. Every order is prepared with intention — from the first espresso pull to the last round of buna. We source responsibly, brew carefully, and serve you with genuine warmth.

When you walk into ETHIOBUNA, you are our guest. Not a transaction.
